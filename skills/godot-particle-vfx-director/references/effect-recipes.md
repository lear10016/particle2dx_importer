# Effect Recipes

Use these recipes as starting structures, then adapt them to the effect brief.

## Hero slash

References: `electro_blade_3d`, `trail`, `trail2`, `ray`, `ribbon`

Layer stack:

- narrow bright core arc
- wider soft afterimage arc
- directional spark streaks
- brief contact burst at the swing end
- optional smoke or mist tail

Polish notes:

- keep the core thin and fast
- let the soft afterimage linger 20 to 40 percent longer
- bias particle direction along the swing path, not evenly radial

## Magic projectile impact

References: `boom`, `ring`, `radial_glow`, `glaxy`, `star_boom`

Layer stack:

- pre-impact charge core
- single-frame flash or near-single-frame flash
- expanding ring or shock disc
- hot spark breakup
- soft fade-out residue

Polish notes:

- the flash should peak before the ring reaches full size
- use additive on the hot center and alpha on the residue
- avoid keeping all layers centered for the full lifetime; let some layers drift

## Channeling aura or charge-up

References: `whirlpool`, `sun_eat`, `ring2`, `light`, `firefly`

Layer stack:

- pulsing center or orbiting seed particles
- one or two rotating rings
- upward drifting embers or motes
- intermittent accent sparks
- optional floor or feet contact glow

Polish notes:

- never let every layer pulse on the same beat
- rotate one layer clockwise and another more slowly or in the opposite direction
- keep the brightest values near the center so the silhouette stays readable

## Fire or explosion burst

References: `flame`, `boom_star`, `gold_boom`, `smoke_fog`, `glow`

Layer stack:

- hot white or yellow ignition core
- orange body mass
- ember breakup
- smoke or soot tail
- optional ground ring or reflected glow

Polish notes:

- the bright core should die early
- smoke should expand and soften after the hot phase, not at the exact same time
- keep the smoke darker and less saturated than the flame body

## Pickup or reward pop

References: `upgrade`, `gold`, `light_face`, `star5`, `radial_glow`

Layer stack:

- compact glow pulse
- upward stars or shards
- quick ring or wave accent
- one lingering twinkle pass

Polish notes:

- keep the whole effect short and optimistic
- let one or two particles overshoot upward to avoid a stiff stop
- use cleaner shapes than combat VFX

## Ambient magic source or shrine loop

References: `glaxy`, `technology`, `fountain`, `smoke_fog`, `wave`

Layer stack:

- looping core glow
- slow orbit or circulating particles
- occasional emission accents
- ambient drift or haze
- ground ripple or energy bed

Polish notes:

- build for long-loop readability, not only the first second
- stagger emission with subtle randomness so it does not look like a metronome
- lower particle count before lowering shape variety
